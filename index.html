Our Sacred Mission

Subulus Salaam Academy is a center for holistic Islamic education. We go beyond memorization to provide a deep understanding of the Deen, covering Jurisprudence (Fiqh), Prophetic Traditions (Hadith), Theology (Tauhid), and Arabic Linguistics.

Our curriculum is designed to mold well-rounded individuals who combine academic excellence with spiritual discipline. We aim to produce knowledgeable Muslims who understand the application of the Qur'an and Sunnah in their daily lives.

Our Programs

Structured academic paths for every stage of your child's journey.

Nursery Category

Nurturing the love of Allah in the youngest hearts. Focusing on oral Hifdh, Islamic ethics (Akhlaq), and Arabic alphabets.

  • Ages 3 - 5
  • Play-based Learning

Primary Category

Building a solid foundation. Systematic memorization of the Qur'an, Tajweed rules, and introduction to Fiqh and Hadith.

  • Ages 6 - 11
  • Structured Curriculum

Secondary Category

Mastery and leadership. Advanced Hifdh completion, Tafsir, Arabic grammar (Nahwu/Sarf), and community service.

  • Ages 12+
  • Advanced Islamic Sciences
